This Privacy Policy describes how the **SSL Check Indicator** extension and API handles your data. We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about our data practices.

## Data Collection

We operate on a principle of data minimization and do not collect personal information unless absolutely necessary for the functionality of the service.

- **No Personal Data:** We do not collect names, email addresses, or other personally identifiable information.
- **No Tracking:** We do not track your browsing history or usage patterns across other websites.
- **Log Data:** Our API server may temporarily log IP addresses and request details for security, rate limiting, and debugging purposes. These logs are regularly rotated and are not used for tracking users.

## How We Use Data

Any data processed by the extension or API is used solely for the purpose of providing the service:

1.  **Certificate Analysis:** The extension sends the hostname of the website you visit to our API to fetch SSL/TLS certificate details.
2.  **Service Improvements:** Aggregated, anonymous usage metrics may be used to identify performance issues and improve the service.

## Data Sharing and Disclosure

We respect your privacy and do not sell or share your data with third parties.

- **We Do Not Sell Data:** We do not sell, trade, or rent your data to advertisers or other third parties.
- **Third-Party Services:** The extension may interact with third-party APIs (e.g., certificate transparency logs) directly to validate certificates, but no user-identifiable data is shared in this process.
- **Legal Requirements:** We may disclose information if required by law or in response to valid requests by public authorities.

## Security

We take reasonable measures to protect the information processed by our service from unauthorized access, alteration, or destruction. All communication between the extension and our API is encrypted via HTTPS.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.

## Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please open an issue in our GitHub repository.
