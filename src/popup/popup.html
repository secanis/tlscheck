TLS 1.3 = good, TLS 1.2 = warn, TLS 1.1/1.0 = bad

Cipher: GCM/CHACHA20 = good, CBC = warn, RC4/3DES/DES/MD5/SHA1/NULL/EXPORT/ANON = bad

Validity: 30+ days = good, <30 days = warn, <7 days or expired = bad

Sources: OWASP TLS Cheat Sheet, Mozilla SSL Config, Qualys SSL Labs guidance.
